OMAR MAI SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED

OMAR MAI SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED is a leading company in the water purification industry, committed to providing affordable, high-quality water purification solutions. Established in June 2024, our mission is to deliver clean and safe drinking water to communities across the country through innovative and sustainable products. With a robust turnover of 100 crores, we have established a strong market presence, reflecting our dedication to meeting customer needs and maintaining high standards of service.

Our company prioritizes customer satisfaction, integrity, and ethical business practices. We leverage advanced technology, including smart purifiers, to enhance our offerings and meet the evolving needs of our customers. As we continue to grow, we are focused on building trust, expanding our market reach, and making a positive impact on the lives of our customers by ensuring access to safe drinking water for all.
